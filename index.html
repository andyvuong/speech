About

This visualization attempts to explore the relationship between the sentiment, volume and pitch of famous speeches. By exploring different speakers, we hope to discover the characteristics of great speeches, patterns in the way speakers talk, and the differences in the way speakers present their speeches.

Created @ UIUC: Jing Huang, Shawn Kollesar, Kanishk Thareja, Andy Vuong, Ji Yang
